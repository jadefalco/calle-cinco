About Calle Cinco Driving School

Calle Cinco's passion is teaching! We believe that learning to drive should be a positive, supportive experience that builds confidence and safety.

Our Approach

  • Patient Instruction: We understand that every student learns differently. Our instructors take the time to work at your pace.
  • Personalized Learning: Each lesson is tailored to your specific needs and goals, whether you're a nervous beginner or refining advanced skills.
  • Safety First: We emphasize responsible driving habits and safety practices that will serve you for life.
  • Proven Results: Our students have a 94% first-try pass rate on their ICBC road test.

Our Instructors

Our team of experienced, patient instructors are dedicated to helping you succeed. They know Kelowna's roads and ICBC test routes inside and out, giving you the local knowledge you need to pass with confidence.
