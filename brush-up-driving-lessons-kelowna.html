What to Expect

What Happens in a Brush-Up Lesson

We start where you are. No condescending basics if you don't need them—just targeted help where you've lost confidence.

Skill Assessment

Your instructor quietly observes your driving for the first 15 minutes to identify what's solid and what needs work.

Targeted Practice

We focus on the specific skills you've forgotten or feel unsure about—parallel parking, highway merging, or roundabout navigation.

Kelowna-Specific Refresher

Get comfortable with local traffic patterns, busy intersections like Harvey and Cooper, and Okanagan highway driving.

Defensive Driving Tune-Up

Relearn hazard perception, safe following distances, and how to anticipate other drivers' mistakes before they happen.

Speed & Control Confidence

Rebuild your feel for the vehicle—smooth braking, confident acceleration, and precise steering in tight spaces.

Anxiety Management

If driving now makes you nervous, we'll work through it gently. Many brush-up students are more anxious than beginners.
