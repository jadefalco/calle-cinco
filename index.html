Frequently Asked Questions

How many lessons do I need?

Most beginners need 7–11 lessons. We create a personalized plan after your first lesson.

Do you provide a vehicle?

Yes! All lessons include our clean, dual-control training car. No need to use your own vehicle.

Can I use your car for my road test?

Absolutely — included in the 11-lesson package and available for separate rental.

Do you work with beginners and nervous drivers?

Yes! We specialize in helping anxious new drivers build real confidence in a calm, supportive environment.

What areas do you serve?

All of Kelowna, West Kelowna, Lake Country, Rutland, and the greater Okanagan.
