Questions Nervous Drivers Ask

I'm extremely nervous. Will you get frustrated with me?

Never. We specialize in anxious drivers. Our instructors are specifically chosen for their patience and calm demeanor. We celebrate small wins and never rush you.

How many lessons until I'm ready for the test?

Most complete beginners need 7-10 lessons to feel fully confident and pass. We don't push you to test until YOU feel ready. It's better to take a few extra lessons than fail and pay retest fees.

Can I use your car for the actual ICBC road test?

Absolutely and we recommend it. Our cars are dual-control, impeccably maintained, and familiar to you by test day. Included in our Complete Package or available for separate rental.

What if I fail my road test?

With our training, it's rare (94% pass first time). But if you do fail, we'll analyze exactly what happened and create a plan to fix it. Usually just 1-2 lessons are needed to address test-day nerves.
