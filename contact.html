Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I start lessons?

Most students book their first lesson within 2–5 days of contacting us. Same-week availability is common, especially if you're flexible on timing.

Do you offer evening or weekend lessons?

Yes. We teach 7 days a week from 7 AM to 8 PM. Evening and weekend slots fill up fastest, so book early if you need those times.

What areas of Kelowna do you cover?

We cover all of Kelowna including Rutland, Glenmore, Dilworth, and West Kelowna. Lessons typically start from your home, school, or workplace when convenient.

Can I use your car for my actual ICBC road test?

Absolutely. Our dual-control vehicle is available for your test day and is included in our Complete Package. You can also rent it separately if needed.
